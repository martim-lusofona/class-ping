Key Features

Dynamic OTP System

Professors display auto-refreshing OTP codes that change at regular intervals, ensuring maximum security and preventing unauthorized attendance marking.

Student Verification

Students confirm their presence by entering the displayed OTP, creating a foolproof system that verifies actual attendance in real-time.

Secure & Reliable

Built with security in mind, our system prevents proxy attendance and ensures that only students physically present can mark their attendance.

Real-time Updates

Attendance data is updated instantly, providing professors with immediate insights into class participation and attendance patterns.

Analytics Dashboard (soon)

Comprehensive reporting and analytics help track attendance trends, identify patterns, and generate detailed reports for academic purposes.

Mobile Friendly

Responsive design ensures seamless experience across all devices, allowing students to mark attendance from any smartphone or tablet.

How It Works

1

Professor Displays OTP

The professor projects a dynamic OTP code on the classroom screen that automatically refreshes every 30 seconds.

2

Students Enter OTP

Students use their devices to enter the current OTP code, confirming their physical presence in the classroom.

3

Attendance Verified

The system verifies the OTP and automatically marks the student as present, creating a secure attendance record.
